Valorant agents, who are they?

Agents are the playable characters in VALORANT ®, representing an agent of the VALORANT Protocol. Each agent serves as a different class with four abilities and has a unique Contract. Abilities are tools that are available to be used by agents in addition to the weapons that they carry. Each ability in VALORANT is unique, though some share similarities.
All agents have three types of abilities:

  • Basic
  • Signature
  • Ultimate

4 abilities in total
Agents have a base health of 100; Light Shields bring the maximum health to 125, and Heavy Shields to 150.
